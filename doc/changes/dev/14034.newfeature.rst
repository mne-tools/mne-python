Added a ``weighted`` option to :func:`mne.chpi.compute_head_pos` that fits all cHPI coils simultaneously with per-coil weights derived from their goodness of fit and inter-coil distance error, avoiding discontinuous jumps ("teleporting") in the estimated head position caused by coils switching in and out of the best-three subset (:gh:`11330`). The default will change from ``False`` to ``True`` in version 1.14; until then, leaving it unset emits a :class:`FutureWarning`. By `Eric Larson`_.
