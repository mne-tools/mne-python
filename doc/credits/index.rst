=======
Credits
=======

MNE-Python was created in 2010 by `Alex Gramfort`_ as a port of :doc:`MNE <../install/mne_c>` software (originally written in C by `Matti Hämäläinen`_).
Development is led by a (mostly volunteer) community of neuroscientists at various academic institutions around the world.
Read more about the current :ref:`leadership team <governance-people>`, the :ref:`contributors <contributors>`,
and the :ref:`supporting institutions and sponsors <funding>` that together make up the MNE-Python ecosystem.
