Credits
=======

MNE-Python was created by `Alex Gramfort`_ as a port of Matti Hämäläinen's :doc:`MNE <../install/mne_c>` software (written in C).
Development is led by a (mostly volunteer) community of neuroscientists at various academic institutions around the world.
Read more about the `leadership team <governance-people>`_, the `contributors`_,
and the `supporting institutions and sponsors <funding>`_ that together make up the MNE-Python ecosystem.
